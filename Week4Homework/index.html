Point Defiance Park

Signed over in 1888 by president Grover Cleveland, Point Defiance Park has a little bit for everybody. There is a beautiful beach with a great view of Mount Ranier, multiple playgrounds for the little ones, and dense old growth trees. If you're ever in the South Sound, Point Defiance is a must-see!

My Personal Experience

2 Years ago I moved to Washington State from southern California, and I was scared of leaving the densely populated area i grew up in. There was so much to do, and so much to see, but point Defiance has offered me a different perspective, as there is always something to do there. I have 3 kids and we have explored up and down this park and have yet to get bored of it's attractions.

Places you must visit in Point Defiance

  1. Owen Beach
  2. Point Defiance Zoo and Aquarium
  3. Chutes and Ladders
  4. Rose Garden

What is there to do?

  • Hiking
  • Cycling
  • Kayaking
